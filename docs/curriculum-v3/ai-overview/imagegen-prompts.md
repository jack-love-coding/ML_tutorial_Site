Eleven files are currently project-bound: the hero, `score-prediction`, and nine prompt-only continuation cards through `robot-control`. The built-in generations endpoint returned network errors before producing `traffic-signals`; under the revised acceptance it is recorded as `deferred`, and its entry remains a pre-recorded generation specification rather than a fabricated or placeholder asset.
